Overview

CyberIntel Corp provides advanced Incident Response and Digital Forensics services designed to detect, contain, eradicate, and remediate sophisticated cyber threats. Our specialists respond rapidly to security breaches, ransomware attacks, insider threats, and advanced persistent threats (APTs), ensuring minimal business disruption and maximum evidence preservation.

Through structured forensic methodologies, we collect and analyze digital evidence from endpoints, servers, network infrastructure, and cloud environments. Our investigations identify attack vectors, lateral movement techniques, persistence mechanisms, and data exfiltration activities. By aligning with recognized industry frameworks such as the NIST Incident Response lifecycle and adversary mapping through the MITRE ATT&CK framework, we provide defensible findings and actionable remediation strategies.

24/7 Rapid Response Capability: Our team is prepared to respond immediately to active cyber incidents to limit damage and accelerate recovery.

Our Incident Response Process

Identification

Detect Abnormal Activity and Confirm Compromise

We continuously monitor systems, networks, endpoints, and cloud environments to identify suspicious activity and potential compromise indicators.

  • Unusual login attempts or privilege escalation
  • Unauthorized access to sensitive data
  • Malware or ransomware indicators
  • Abnormal traffic patterns
  • Insider threat behavior

Containment

Isolate Affected Systems to Prevent Spread

CyberIntel Corp’s rapid response team acts quickly to isolate compromised systems and prevent attackers from moving laterally across the network environment.

Long-Term Containment

  • Temporary firewall rule implementation
  • Network segmentation adjustments
  • Security patch deployment
  • Enhanced monitoring and threat detection controls

Eradication

Remove Malware and Attacker Persistence Mechanisms

The Eradication phase focuses on completely eliminating the threat from the affected environment. After containment has stopped the spread of the attack, CyberIntel Corp specialists work to remove malware, unauthorized access, and attacker persistence mechanisms.

Comprehensive Threat Removal

  • Removing malware, ransomware payloads, and malicious scripts
  • Deleting unauthorized user accounts and access tokens
  • Cleaning malicious registry keys and scheduled tasks
  • Resetting compromised credentials and privileged accounts

Recovery

Restore Systems Safely and Monitor for Reinfection

The Recovery phase focuses on safely restoring affected systems and returning business operations to normal while ensuring the environment remains secure. After eradication confirms that threats have been removed, CyberIntel Corp carefully reintroduces systems into production under controlled conditions.

Secure System Restoration

  • Restoration from verified, uncompromised backups
  • Reinstallation of operating systems when required
  • Secure configuration hardening
  • Credential resets for affected users and administrators

Post-Incident Review

Strengthen Defenses and Prevent Recurrence

The goal is to ensure future incidents are prevented, response time is improved, and overall resilience is strengthened.

Review & Analysis

  • Timeline of the attack from initial compromise to detection
  • How attackers gained access and moved laterally
  • Effectiveness of detection and response measures
  • Gaps in monitoring, controls, or policies

Digital Forensics Capabilities

  • Disk & Memory Forensics
  • Log and Event Analysis
  • Network Traffic Investigation
  • Ransomware Analysis
  • Chain of Custody Documentation

Industry Frameworks

CyberIntel Corp leverages globally recognized cybersecurity frameworks, including the NIST Cybersecurity Framework, MITRE ATT&CK, and ISO/IEC 27035, to deliver structured, repeatable, and auditable incident response. These frameworks guide our detection, containment, eradication, and recovery processes, ensuring threats are managed efficiently. By mapping adversary tactics and aligning with international standards, we strengthen defenses and maintain compliance with regulatory requirements. This approach enables CyberIntel Corp to provide proactive, defensible, and enterprise-grade cyber threat management for modern organizations.
